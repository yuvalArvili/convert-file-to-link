Rhodes, Greece! A beautiful city with a rich history and stunning architecture. Here are three popular tourist attractions to add to your itinerary:

Top Attractions

1.
The Acropolis of Rhodes: This ancient acropolis sits atop a hill, offering breathtaking views of the city and the sea. Explore the remains of the Temple of Apollo, the Stadium, and the Theater, and soak up the atmosphere of this historic site.
2.
The Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes: This impressive palace is a masterpiece of Gothic architecture, with ornate stone carvings, grand halls, and beautiful gardens. Learn about the history of the Knights of Rhodes and the palace's role in the city's rich past.
3.
The Street of the Knights (Ippoton Street): Take a stroll down this cobblestone street, lined with impressive medieval buildings, some of which date back to the 14th century. Admire the beautiful doorways, ornate facades, and picturesque courtyards, and get a glimpse into the lives of the Knights of Rhodes. Enjoy your time in Rhodes!
