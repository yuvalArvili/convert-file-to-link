Abu Dhabi! You'll have a fantastic time in this modern, cosmopolitan city with a rich cultural heritage. Here are three popular tourist attractions in Abu Dhabi, with a short description, and an extra emphasis on kid-friendly activities:

Top Attractions

1.
Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi: This indoor theme park is a dream come true for kids! With 29 thrilling rides, shows, and attractions based on popular Warner Bros. characters, your little ones will be thrilled to meet Bugs Bunny, Scooby-Doo, and friends. The park is fully air-conditioned, making it a great spot to escape the sun.
2.
Ferrari World Abu Dhabi: Get ready for an adrenaline rush! This iconic theme park is the world's first Ferrari-branded park, featuring record-breaking roller coasters, including the world's fastest (0-240 km/h in 4.9 seconds!). There are also gentler rides for younger kids. Don't miss the Ferrari museum and interactive exhibits.
3.
Emirates Park Zoo: Spread over 1.4 million square kilometers, this zoo is home to over 1,700 animals from around the world, including elephants, giraffes, and even a petting zoo for kids. Take a guided tour or enjoy the animal shows and feeding sessions. The zoo also offers a fun, interactive 'Edutainment' area for kids to learn about conservation and wildlife. Additional suggestions for kids: * **Legoland Water Park**: A water park with rides, slides, and play areas designed for kids aged 2-12. * **Khalifa Park**: A large public park with playgrounds, picnic areas, and a lake with paddle boat rentals. * **Sea Life**: An aquarium featuring a vast array of fish, sharks, and even a walk-through underwater tunnel. Have a fantastic time in Abu Dhabi with your family!
