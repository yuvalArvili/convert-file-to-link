Amsterdam! Here are 3 popular tourist attractions for you to consider: **1. Anne Frank House** Visit the historic canal house where the Jewish girl Anne Frank wrote her famous diary during WWII. The museum features a reconstructed version of the secret annex, as well as exhibits on the Frank family's life in hiding. **2. Rijksmuseum** Discover an impressive collection of Dutch art and history at this iconic museum. With over 8,000 objects on display, including works by Rembrandt, Vermeer, and other Dutch Masters, you'll be immersed in the country's rich cultural heritage. **3. Vondelpark** Escape the hustle and bustle of the city center in this beautiful and peaceful park. Take a leisurely stroll, rent a bike, or enjoy a picnic on the grass, surrounded by lush greenery and scenic ponds. Enjoy your trip to Amsterdam!
