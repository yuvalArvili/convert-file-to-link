Rhodes, Greece! A beautiful city with a rich cultural heritage. Here are three popular tourist attractions in Rhodes, each with a short description:

Top Attractions

1.
The Acropolis of Rhodes): This ancient acropolis sits atop a hill, offering breathtaking views of the city and the Aegean Sea. Explore the remains of the Temple of Apollo, the Stadium of Rhodes, and the Theater of Rhodes, all dating back to the 2nd century BC.
2.
The Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes: This magnificent palace was built in the 14th century by the Knights Hospitaller and later converted into a palace by the Grand Master. Admire the stunning Gothic architecture, ornate mosaics, and beautifully restored courtyards. **Note:** The palace is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
