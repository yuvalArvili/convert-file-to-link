Rhodes, Greece! A magnificent city with a blend of ancient ruins, stunning beaches, and a rich history. Here are three popular tourist attractions in Rhodes, with a short description each: **1. Acropolis of Rhodes** Explore the ancient Acropolis, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, which dates back to the 3rd century BC. The acropolis offers breathtaking views of the city and the sea. Kids will love the scenic hike up to the top, and the stunning vistas will leave everyone in awe. **2. Palace of the Grand Master of the Knights of Rhodes** Step into the impressive Palace of the Grand Master, a well-preserved medieval castle from the 14th century. The palace features an impressive courtyard, ornate decorations, and a fascinating history. Kids will enjoy the interactive displays and the opportunity to imagine life in a medieval castle. **3. Elli Beach** Unwind at Elli Beach, a popular spot for families and kids. This picturesque beach offers calm waters, sunbeds, and umbrellas for a relaxing day out. Kids can enjoy swimming, building sandcastles, or trying water sports like paddleboarding or kayaking. Additional kid-friendly attractions in Rhodes: * Rhodes Aquarium: Explore the marine life of the Aegean Sea. * Rhodes Old Town: Get lost in the narrow streets and discover hidden gems, including the famous Street of the Knights. * Water Park: A fun-filled day with water slides, pools, and games for kids of all ages. Remember to plan your day according to the sun and heat, and don't forget to try some delicious Greek cuisine!
